Work

Currently a Post-doctoral researcher at the Great Lakes Laboratory for Fisheries and Aquatic Sciences, part of Fisheries & Oceans Canada. My current projects revolve around utilizing remote sensing to assist fish & fish habitat research and management in freshwater lakes of Canada. If you're interested in my publications and other experience, check out my CV tab.

My work has five components: A Canadian Technical Report of Fisheries and Aquatic Sciences "POtential use of remote sensing to support the management of freshwater fish habitat in Canada" (reference coming soon), a Google Earth Engine App to map ice in the Great Lakes coastal zone (publication submitted), wetland mapping to identify changes in fish habitat following management, physical habitat mapping in clear freshwater lakes, and remote sensing investigations for landscape-level changes in critical fish habitat.

About

I am an individual with a drive to develop useful tools, conduct novel research, and advance our understanding of aquatic systems and resources by melding biology and geography.
